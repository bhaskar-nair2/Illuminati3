FAQ

How do I register ?

Head over to the Register link from the navigation or head over to the registration desks at UB / JAVA Green.

Who can participate ?

Only for first years of SRM University.

Is it an individual or team challenge?

It's strictly a team challenge of 2 members.

Is there any registration fee ?

Yes and the registration fee is Rs. 100/team.

Where to pay ?

Payment has to be made at the registration desk.
