FAQ

How do I register ?

Go to the menu on the top right corner, select the Register tab and you will be redirected to the registration page. After registration you can add your second team member.

Who can participate ?

Only for the students in their first year in SRM University.

Is it an individual or team challenge?

It's strictly a team challenge of 2 members.

For queries and updates ?

Do check out our Facebook page for updates and for queries you can message us on Facebook or mail us at support@sweassociation.in

Is there any registration fee ?

Yes, the registration fee is Rs 100 per team.

Where do I pay ?

Payment can be made at the registeration desks set up at UB, Java or at the Hostels.

How to login?

Login with your Team Name and the mobile number which is registered. Moreover Team Member can be added from the panel after login and if the information set is not right, it can be updated using the edit icon.
